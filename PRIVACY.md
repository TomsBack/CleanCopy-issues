# CleanCopy Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

CleanCopy does not collect, sell, share, transmit, or store personal data outside your browser.

CleanCopy runs locally in Chrome to remove tracking parameters and redirect wrappers from URLs. The extension may read the current page URL, a link URL you right-click, or a copied URL when you enable optional auto-clean-on-copy. This processing happens on your device.

## Data Stored Locally

CleanCopy stores settings in Chrome storage, including enabled cleaning categories, custom parameters, and notification preferences. These settings stay in your browser unless you use browser sync features controlled by Chrome.

## Data Collection

CleanCopy does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, external tracking, remote logging, or external servers. It does not transmit browsing history, page content, copied URLs, or settings to the developer or any third party.

## Permissions

CleanCopy requests browser permissions only to provide its URL-cleaning features: context menu actions, keyboard shortcuts, clipboard writing, local notifications, local settings storage, and optional copy-event handling on pages.
